Govt bans Jugnu & 4 other OTT platforms over obscene content

PS
Pragya Swastik
The Verge 12:05 pm on 27 Feb 2026

The Ministry of Information and Broadcasting has issued an order banning five OTT platforms, including Jugnu, for streaming obscene and vulgar content that violates the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021.

The decision comes after several complaints were filed by users and civil society organizations highlighting the availability of content that goes against Indian cultural values and existing broadcasting standards.

Industry experts say this move could have far-reaching implications for content regulation in the digital streaming space, as the government continues to tighten oversight of online media platforms operating in India.
