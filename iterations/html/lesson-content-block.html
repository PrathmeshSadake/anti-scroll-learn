What are LLMs?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are specialized AI models trained on vast amounts of text data.

🔧

Scale is Key

LLMs achieve their capabilities through enormous scale—often billions or trillions of parameters.

⚡

The Transformer

Most modern LLMs are based on the Transformer architecture introduced by Google in 2017.

👁️

Attention is All You Need

Attention allows the model to weigh the importance of different words.
